🪂 Paragliden boven Medellín

Medellín · Colombia

Medellín en de omgeving staan bekend als een van de beste paragliding-locaties van Zuid-Amerika. De thermiek boven de Andese valleien is uitstekend en de uitzichten over de stad en het omringende groen zijn spectaculair. Je vliegt altijd tandem met een gecertificeerde gids.

De meeste vluchtlocaties liggen net buiten de stad op ±30-45 minuten met Uber. De vlucht duurt zelf 15-30 minuten afhankelijk van de wind. Geen ervaring nodig — de gids doet alles. Dit is ongetwijfeld de meest spectaculaire manier om het landschap rond Medellín te ervaren.

Paragliding the Valle de Aburrá Medellín is striking from any angle, but for the very best views of the city take to the skies on the thermal winds above the Valle de Aburrá to enjoy uninterrupted panoramas on a tandem paragliding flight. With an experienced pilot manning the controls, visitors are free to sit back in the harness and take in the sights while floating through the air with just the occasional bird of prey for company. Flights take off from the chilly little village of San Felix, which is perched high up in the mountains on the western side of the valley. San Felix is famed for its cheese-filled panchocha bread. Depending on the weather conditions, the flights will either land in the same spot or down below in the municipality of Bello. From the launching point, it is possible to make out parts of Medellín. However, once you’re up in the air, the entire metropolitan area opens out before your eyes with the city appearing diminutive and tranquil in the embrace of the imposing mountains. Keep an eye out for the Coltejer Tower, which marks the center of the city, while in the distance the towers of El Poblado and Envigado look like enoki mushrooms sprouting from the slopes. There are a number of paragliding operators in the village. Zona de Vuelo is one of the most experienced operators and offers daily flights, weather permitting. It’s quite a hike up to the launch point, so it’s a good idea to call ahead to check on conditions before setting out. Chicamocha Paragliding Another fantastic place to try paragliding is above the magnificent Cañon del Chicamocha outside San Gil in the department of Santander, where participants soar over an immense arid river valley. Getting Around Medellín’s innovative mass transit system is the pride of the paisas and the envy of every other city in the country. Having your own wheels here is more often a hindrance than a benefit. The backbone of the network is the two-line metro. A series of five cable-car lines branch out from metro stations to serve the densely populated neighborhoods on the mountainsides, while a single tram line runs to the east of the city center. With one ticket, it’s possible to change between services and travel from one extreme of the city to another.

💡 Tips

• Boek via je hotel voor een betrouwbare gecertificeerde operator

• Beste periode: droog seizoen (december-maart, juli-augustus) voor betere zichtbaarheid

• Neem het vlieggoed dat de gids aanraadt — het kan koud zijn boven op de heuvel

• Laat foto/video maken door de gids (extra kost) — de beelden zijn spectaculair

• Combineer met downhill mountainbiken op dezelfde dag voor een volledig avontuurdag
